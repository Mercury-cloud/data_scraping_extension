What are Collections?

Data that you scrape is saved for you into collections. You can View, ( Download) or ( Delete) a collection.

How to scrape a list of URLs?

Data Miner can open a list of URLs you provide in a CSV file and scrape each and store the data into a single collection. Examples: Facebook , AngelList
